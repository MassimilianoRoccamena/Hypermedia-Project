Child4Help is a humanitarian association whose fundamental aim is to provide help to needy children around the world.
The association is committed to providing a series of services that guarantee important possibilities for children in need such as the possibility to study or to have a welcoming home. To increase the community's awareness of these services Child4Help is also committed to organizing events for anyone who wants to help do their part.
From USA to Kenya, Child4Help’s objectives are the same: Go wherever we’re needed — and do whatever it takes — to ensure children grow up healthy, learning and safe.
