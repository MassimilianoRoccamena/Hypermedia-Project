Kenya Headquarter

Kenya is the main place where our association has been operating for many years now. This headquarters was founded in 2018 to provide children in nearby villages with a place of reference they can count on. it is equipped with every medical necessity and classes to enable children to have an education. it also has a housing section aimed at providing a home for those children who do not even have a roof over their heads.

Location: ragati road n.5, Nairobi-Kenya
